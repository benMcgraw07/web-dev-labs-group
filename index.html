Hulk: The Green Titan

Who is the Hulk?

The Hulk, created by Stan Lee and Jack Kirby, first smashed onto the scene in The Incredible Hulk #1 in May 1962. Dr. Bruce Banner, a brilliant scientist, transforms into a massive, green-skinned behemoth when angered, thanks to a gamma radiation accident. Known as the "Green Goliath," the Hulk is one of Marvel Comics’ strongest heroes, balancing raw power with inner turmoil.

The character’s journey spans comics, TV, films, and more, evolving from a misunderstood monster to a complex hero. His rage-driven strength makes him both a savior and a threat, often clashing with heroes like the Avengers or villains like the Abomination.
